Chicken Alfredo

Description

Chicken Alfredo is a staple in my househould. This was one of the first dishes that I learned how to cook when I first mooved out. This is my favorite way to prepare mine that is great when you are on a time crunch.

Ingredients

  • 3 medium sized chicken breasts
  • Olive oil
  • Panko bread crumbs
  • 1 large egg
  • Pasta of your choice
  • Salt and Pepper to taste
  • Winco garlic and herbs alfredo sauce
  • Garlic butter
  • French bread loaf

Steps

  1. Cut up chicken breasts into longer strips
  2. Crack egg into bowl and soak chicken breasts into mixture
  3. Place breadcrumbs onto a plate and cover chicken pieces
  4. Heat up some olive oil in a pan and cook chicken pieces until golden brown
  5. Meanwhile, boil some pasta in a pot until al dente
  6. Drain the pasta and add to a saucepan along with the alfredo sauce jar
  7. Add cooked chicken strips and mix into the pasta
  8. Toast the garlic butter on the french bread and serve with the pasta
